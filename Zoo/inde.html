What is Zooper?

Zooper is a page created by my Mario Ocampo in order to make easy access to five of the best zoos in the world, and to create a community for and against animal abuse worldwide, Zooper is a non-profit organization profit in search of the social acceptance of zoos showing the points in favor and demonstrating that in these care centers the animals have the necessary care to guarantee the optimal development and prioritize the freedom of thousands of animals.
